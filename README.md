# kali-paradevi

A new symbolic system built on real astronomy. Not a zodiac. Not a horoscope. A mirror made of the actual sky.

**kali-paradevi** is an adaptive astrology for the [recursive.eco](https://recursive.eco) platform. Where inherited astrology asks the sky to predict you, this system asks the sky to reflect you. Every card is a real object — a black hole with a catalog number, a galaxy with a measured distance — carrying a reflective layer of themes and prompts on top of verified astronomy. The facts are science; the meaning is yours to make. This is the same move the sibling recursive-tarot project makes with historical decks: game before belief, mirror before oracle, honesty about what the thing is.

The system has two faces that are one body. **Kali** is the dark face: black holes, voids, mergers, the unseen mass that bends everything — dissolution, endings, the devouring of time. **Paradevi** is the light face: galaxies, star nurseries, radiant structure — manifestation, abundance, the shining of consciousness. In the nondual Śaiva tradition the builder studies (as taught publicly by Christopher Wallis), Kālī and Parā are not two goddesses in a hierarchy but "two sides of the same coin... aspects of each other" — and, remarkably, Wallis himself has reached for the same image in a public satsang: Kali as the black hole, Parā as the white hole (see `research/wallis-grounding.md`). Every Kali card in this system is linked to a Paradevi counterpart by an `emanation` cross-reference. Flip the card, and the void shows you its radiance; flip it back, and the radiance shows you its void.

Grief and play coexist here. A supermassive black hole sits at the heart of our own galaxy's light — that is not a metaphor we invented, it is where we live.

## Dual-mode architecture

| | Kali | Paradevi |
|---|---|---|
| UI register | dark mode | light mode |
| Sky | black holes, voids, dark mass | galaxies, clusters, radiant structure |
| Imagery | EHT shadows, void maps, gravitational-wave events | Hubble / JWST / ESO radiance |
| Reading | dissolution, shadow, what devours | manifestation, light, what shines |
| Relation | each item carries an `emanation` link to its counterpart in the other mode — the flip is the practice |

One system, two lenses. Content varies by mode; the underlying deck is a set of paired items, not a fork.

## What this is NOT

- **Not predictive.** No claims that celestial positions determine personality or fate. The astronomy is real; the meaning-making is explicitly reflective, chosen, and yours.
- **Not inherited zodiacal doctrine.** No signs, no houses, no birth charts. The objects are selected for what they actually are, not for where they sit relative to Earth's ecliptic.
- **Not an authority.** The system never claims to know you better than you know yourself (the non-consent axiom). A card is an invitation to look, never a verdict.
- **Not a representation of Śaiva Tantra.** The Kali/Paradevi naming honors a relationship the builder studies; the doctrinal grounding is kept in attributed study notes, and the system itself makes no claim to teach the tradition.

## Platform plan (4 layers)

1. **Repo** (this) — canonical open home: design docs, research, object data, journal.
2. **Grammars** — the paired decks as recursive.eco grammar JSON (`grammars/`), items with real astronomy + reflective layer + images.
3. **Channel** — a kali-paradevi channel on recursive.eco collecting the grammars and related tools.
4. **Casting** — interactive draws: draw-one-and-flip (the emanation move made playable), a wheel of the galactic neighborhood, and whatever the community invents.
