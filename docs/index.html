How to read

Flip the void, find the radiance

Every dark card is bound 1:1 to a light counterpart. Draw a black hole and its paired galaxy is named on the card; draw a galaxy and its paired void answers back. You read the pair, not the single face — dissolution and manifestation as one motion seen from two sides.

Kali — the dark face

Black holes, voids, mergers, the unseen mass that bends everything. What devours, dissolves, ends, transforms. Each item names its light twin.

Paradevi — the light face

Galaxies, star nurseries, radiant structure. What pours forth, connects, manifests, shines. Each item names its dark twin.

A card is a prompt, not a prophecy: read to know yourself, not to be told your fate.
